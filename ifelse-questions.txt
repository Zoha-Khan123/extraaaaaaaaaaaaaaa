Question: Battery Status Checker
User apni device ki battery percentage enter karega.
Aap ko conditions lagani hain:
Agar battery percentage 80 se zyada ho
→ Message show ho: Battery Full
Agar battery percentage 30 se zyada ho 
→ Message show ho: Battery Medium
Agar battery percentage 30 se kam ho
→ Message show ho: Low Battery! Please Charge


Question 2 – Marks Position

User marks enter kare.
Agar marks 90 se zyada ho → Excellent
Agar marks 70 se zyada ho → Good
Agar marks 50 se zyada ho → Average
Warna → Fail




Question 3 – Simple Password Check
User password enter kare.
Conditions lagao:
Agar password "admin123" ho → Access Granted
Agar password "guest123" ho → Limited Access
Warna → Wrong Password

================== student ==============
And (&&)
Scenario:
School me students ke marks aur attendance ke basis par decide hota hai ki student Pass hoga ya Fail.
Rules:
Marks 40 ya us se zyada honi chahiye
Attendance 75% ya us se zyada honi chahiye
Dono conditions true honi chahiye tabhi student Pass hoga
Agar koi bhi condition false ho → Fail



OR (||) – Koi bhi ek condition sahi ho to chalega
Situation 1 – Weekend School
Agar aaj Saturday hai ya Sunday hai
→ School band hai
Agar weekday hai
→ School khula hai


Example 3 – Free Delivery
Agar order amount 500 ya us se zyada hai ya customer VIP member hai
→ Free delivery milegi
Agar dono condition false hai
→ Delivery charge lagega


NOT (!) – Condition ka opposite
Situation 1 – Rain Check
Agar barish nahi ho rahi hai (!isRaining)
→ Bahar jao
Agar barish ho rahi hai
→ Ghar me raho


Situation 2 – Permission Denied
Agar permission nahi hai (!hasPermission)
→ Access Denied
Agar permission hai
→ Access Granted
